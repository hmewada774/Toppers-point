## ğŸ“ Step-by-Step Guide

### Adding a New Topper:
1. Click on **"Toppers"** tab
2. Fill in the form:
   - Student Name
   - Year
   - Subject
   - Class (8, 9, 10, 11, or 12)
   - Marks (%)
   - Upload photo (click the upload area)
3. Click **"Add Topper"**
4. To feature on homepage, click **"Feature"** button on the topper card

### Adding an Event:
1. Click on **"Events"** tab
2. Fill in:
   - Event Title
   - Year
   - Upload event photo
3. Click **"Add Event"**

### Adding a Video:
1. Click on **"Videos"** tab
2. Choose video type:
   - **Upload Video File**: Select MP4 or WebM file (max 50 MB)
   - **YouTube Link**: Paste YouTube URL
   - **Vimeo Link**: Paste Vimeo URL
3. Fill in title and description
4. Click **"Add Video"**

### Updating Founder Info:
1. Click on **"Founder"** tab
2. Fill in all details:
   - Name, Title, Qualification
   - Contact Number
   - Social Media Links (Instagram, WhatsApp, Facebook)
   - Upload photo (optional)
3. Click **"Save Founder Details"**
